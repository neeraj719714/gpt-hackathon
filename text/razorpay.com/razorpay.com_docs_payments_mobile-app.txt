This app enables you to:

View real-time status on payments made by customers.
Accept payments from your customers using Razorpay.me URL and Payment Links.
Issue refunds to customers.
View and track settlement details.
Generate API keys.

Advantages

Payment and settlement data now at your finger tips.
Razorpay.me URL created instantly after activation.
Failed payment? No problem! Create and share a Payment Link.
Issue refunds in a few taps.

How It Works
Follow these steps:

Install the Razorpay Payments Mobile App on your Android or iOS smartphone.
Log in to the Razorpay Dashboard with your credentials. If you do not have an account, create a new Razorpay account.
You can now perform any of the following operations:

Accept payments from your customers using:


Razorpay.me URL


Payment Links



View status of payments made by customers.
Issue partial or full refund to customers. You can issue instant or regular refunds.
View and track settlements.
Generate API keys.
