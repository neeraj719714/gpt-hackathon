View Settlements Using Dashboard
You can view details of settlements made to you from the Razorpay Dashboard.
You can view a detailed break-down of the amount settled to you from the Dashboard. You can view details, such as the total settlement amount (credit + debit amount), applicable service fees, service tax, adjustments, and so on.
Watch this video to see how to view settlements from the Razorpay Dashboard.

To view settlement details:

Log in to the Razorpay Dashboard.
Navigate to Settlements.
Click on a Settlement id that you wish to refer to.

View Settlement Timeline on Dashboard
You can view the settlement timeline to check when the settlement for a particular payment will be credited.
To view the settlement timeline:

Log in to the Razorpay Dashboard.
Navigate to Transactions and select Payments or Refunds depending on what you wish to view the settlement timeline for.
Select the payment id or the refund id you wish to check.
Click View settlement timeline.


Upon expanding the settlement timeline, you will be able to see the settlement schedule for that particular payment.



Enable Settlements Placed On Hold
Your settlement can be placed on hold if we detect some risk with your payments or with your Razorpay account. You are notified about this on your Dashboard.

To enable settlements when they are put on hold:

Log in to the Razorpay Dashboard.
Navigate to Settlements.
Select Contact Support.
