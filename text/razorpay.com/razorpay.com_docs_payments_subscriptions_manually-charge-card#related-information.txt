Manually Charge a Card From Dashboard
Watch this video to see how to manually charge a card.

To manually charge a card:

Log in to the Razorpay Dashboard and click Subscriptions under PAYMENT PRODUCTS in the left menu.
Click the Subscription Id in the pending state that is to be charged.
Click the invoice in the issued state and click Attempt Charge. Alternatively, you can click Attempt Charge against the invoice in the issued state on the details panel.
